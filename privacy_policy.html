Section 02

Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name — provided during registration or pulled from your Google/Apple sign-in profile.
  • Email address — used for authentication, account recovery, and transactional communications.
  • Authentication identifiers — unique user IDs generated by our authentication system.
  • Profile preferences — such as preferred language, currency, and theme (dark/light).

2.2 Project and Report Data

When you use the Service to create feasibility studies, we collect:

  • Project inputs — business name, industry, country, financial parameters (investment amount, expected revenue, expected costs), market details, operational parameters, legal considerations, and analysis preferences.
  • Generated reports — the AI-generated feasibility analysis results, including scores, narratives, financial projections, risk assessments, and recommendations.
  • Chat messages — messages you send to the AI Chat assistant within a study context, and the AI's responses.

2.3 Subscription and Payment Data

  • Subscription tier and status — Free, Pro, or Team plan level and whether the subscription is active.
  • Payment provider and transaction identifiers — we receive transaction references from RevenueCat (our subscription management service) and the respective app stores. We do not directly collect or store your credit card numbers, bank account details, or other financial payment instruments.

2.4 Usage and Diagnostic Data

  • Feature usage patterns — such as number of studies created, pages visited, and features used.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, app version, and language settings.
  • Crash logs and error reports — technical data to help us identify and fix issues.

2.5 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect precise geolocation, contacts, photos, videos, browsing history, health data, or biometric data.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service — create and store your feasibility studies, generate AI-powered reports, and enable features like project comparison and AI chat.
  • Process AI analysis — send your project inputs to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate feasibility reports. See Section 4 for details.
  • Manage your account — authenticate your identity, manage subscription status, and apply your preferences.
  • Improve the Service — analyze usage patterns to fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features.
  • Communicate with you — send transactional emails related to your account (e.g., password resets). We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in.
  • Enforce our terms — detect abuse, prevent fraud, and ensure compliance with our Terms of Service.
Section 04

How AI Processes Your Data

FeasAI uses the Anthropic Claude API to generate feasibility study reports. When you create a study:

  1. Your project inputs (business details, financial parameters, market information, etc.) are sent to Anthropic's API through our secure backend (Supabase Edge Functions).
  2. Anthropic processes these inputs to generate the feasibility report content.
  3. The generated report is returned to our servers and stored in your account.

Important notes about AI processing:

  • Your project data is sent to Anthropic solely for the purpose of generating your feasibility report.
  • Anthropic's data usage policies apply to the data processed by their API. As of the effective date of this policy, Anthropic states that they do not use API inputs or outputs to train their models. Please refer to Anthropic's privacy policy for their current terms.
  • We do not send your name, email, or other personally identifiable information to Anthropic — only the business/project data needed for analysis.

Section 06

Data Storage & Security

  • Storage: Your data is stored in Supabase-managed PostgreSQL databases with row-level security (RLS) policies ensuring that each user can only access their own data.
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL. Supabase encrypts data at rest.
  • Access controls: Our backend enforces authentication and authorization checks on every request. Admin access to production data is limited and logged.
  • Infrastructure: Supabase infrastructure is hosted on cloud providers with SOC 2 compliance. Please refer to Supabase's security documentation for details.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Section 08

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active and the Service is in use.
  • Deleted studies: When you delete a study, all associated data (inputs, report, chat messages) is permanently deleted from our database.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Backup copies may persist in system backups for up to 90 days before being purged.
  • Inactive accounts: We may delete accounts that have been inactive for more than 24 months, after providing notice to the associated email address.
