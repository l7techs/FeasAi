FAQ

Common questions

FeasAI generates a comprehensive feasibility study report with 9 sections: Executive Summary (with a 0–100 feasibility score), Market Analysis, Financial Analysis, Technical Feasibility, Operational Feasibility, Legal & Regulatory, Risk Assessment, Sensitivity Analysis, and Recommendations. Each section includes professional charts and data visualizations.

Every AI action in FeasAI costs credits. You receive monthly credits through your subscription plan (Free, Pro, or Team). You can also buy top-up packs at any time — with or without a subscription. Subscription credits are consumed first, and top-up credits are only used when monthly credits run out. Subscription credits reset each billing cycle, while top-up credits never expire.

FeasAI is a powerful decision-support tool, but it is not a replacement for professional financial, legal, or consulting advice. The AI-generated reports are based on your inputs and general analysis models. For high-stakes decisions, we recommend using FeasAI as a starting point and consulting qualified professionals.

Your project data is stored securely using Supabase (backed by PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies. When generating reports, your inputs are sent to the Anthropic Claude AI API for analysis. We do not sell your data, and you can export or delete your data at any time from within the app.
