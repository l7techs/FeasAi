Delete Your Data

Request deletion of specific data from your FeasAI account without deleting your entire account.

ℹ Your account stays active. You can request deletion of specific studies, reports, or chat history while keeping your account and remaining data intact.

What data can be deleted

  • Individual studies — project inputs, generated report, and scores
  • AI chat history — follow-up conversations within a study
  • All studies at once — remove every study while keeping your account
  • Profile preferences — reset your language, currency, and theme choices

How to delete your data

1
In-app (recommended): Open any study → tap the menu (⋮) → select "Delete Study" → confirm. This instantly and permanently removes that study and all related data.
2
Via email: If you need help deleting specific data or cannot access the app, send us a request using the button below. Describe which data you'd like deleted and include your account email.

We will process email requests within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.
